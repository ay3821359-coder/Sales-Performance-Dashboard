# 📊 E-Commerce Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an interactive **E-Commerce Sales Dashboard** built using **Microsoft Power BI**. The dashboard provides valuable business insights by analyzing sales performance, customer behavior, product categories, regions, and shipping methods.

The objective of this project is to help business users make data-driven decisions through interactive visualizations and KPIs.

---

## 🚀 Dashboard Features

- 📈 Total Sales KPI
- 📦 Total Orders KPI
- 👥 Total Customers KPI
- 💰 Average Sales KPI
- 📊 Sales by Category
- 🌍 Sales by Region
- 📅 Monthly Sales Trend
- 🛍️ Top 10 Customers
- 📦 Top 10 Sub-Categories
- 🚚 Ship Mode Analysis
- 🥧 Sales Distribution by Segment
- 🎛️ Interactive Region Slicer

---

## 📊 Key Business Insights

- Technology category generated the highest sales.
- West region recorded strong sales performance.
- Standard Class is the most frequently used shipping mode.
- Top customers contribute a significant portion of total revenue.
- Monthly sales trend helps identify business growth patterns.
